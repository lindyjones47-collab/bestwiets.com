## What was fixed

### 1. Product meta descriptions (biggest win — 145 pages)
Product pages were emitting the **entire product description** as the meta tag — up to **1,141 characters** (e.g. AK-47). Google truncates at ~155, so most of it was wasted and looked messy in SERPs.
- Added `product_meta_desc()` in `generate.py` (mirrored by `_metaDesc()` in `index.html` for SPA navigation): builds a concise ~150-char description from the product name + first sentence + delivery line.
- AK-47 went from **1,141 → 154 chars**.

### 2. Landing-page descriptions
Trimmed `/`, `/de/`, `/en/`, and `/prerolls` descriptions to under 160 chars while keeping the primary keywords and payment/delivery hooks.

### 3. Thin descriptions
Expanded `/returns` and `/voorwaarden` descriptions from ~40–55 chars to a useful 120–140.

### 4. Blog title suffix
Shortened `| WietStore Blog` → `| WietStore` across all 38 articles, pulling several titles back under the SERP limit.

## Pre-Rolls category SEO (dedicated work)

The `/prerolls` category landing and its 15 products were audited and upgraded:

- **Title trimmed** to 50 chars: *"Voorgedraaide Joints & Pre-Rolls Kopen | WietStore"*.
- **Description** rewritten to 152 chars, keyword-led ("voorgedraaide joints en pre-rolls kopen").
- **FAQ section added** (4 Q&As) with matching **`FAQPage` JSON-LD** → eligible for FAQ rich results. Visible content is present in both the pre-rendered HTML and the client-rendered page so it stays valid whether or not Google executes JS.
- Landing page now carries **`CollectionPage` + `ItemList` + `FAQPage` + `BreadcrumbList`** structured data.
- **Product variants** (3/12/30 stuks, strain options) surface as **`AggregateOffer`** (lowPrice/highPrice) in each product's `Product` schema, and as "vanaf €X" on cards.
- Every preroll product has a unique title, ~150-char description, self-hosted image with `alt`, breadcrumb, and internal links.
