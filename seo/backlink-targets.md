# Backlink Targets — Netherlands · Belgium · Germany · France

Companion to [backlinks.md](backlinks.md) (strategy + templates). This file is the **concrete target list**.

## ⚠️ Read this first — three honest caveats

1. **Verify every domain before pitching.** Sites get sold, go dark, change their link policy, or turn into spam farms. The named properties below were real and established at the time of writing — but **always check the live site + its current authority** before spending outreach time. The "how to vet" section tells you how.
2. **Cannabis link-building is genuinely hard.** Many mainstream sites won't link to a cannabis *delivery* business (vs. an info site), because selling/shipping cannabis across NL/BE/DE/FR borders is legally grey. The realistic wins are **cannabis-friendly properties**: niche media, advocacy/harm-reduction orgs, directories, forums, and lifestyle blogs — not general news or .gov sites.
3. **Quality over quantity, always.** One relevant link from a real cannabis publication (DR 40+) beats 50 directory links. But you still want the directory floor for legitimacy. Do both, in the order below.

## How to vet a target (do this BEFORE pitching — 2 min each)

Run each candidate through this checklist. Skip any that fail 2+:

1. **Is it live and updated?** Last post within ~6 months. Dead blogs pass no value.
2. **Is it relevant?** Cannabis / lifestyle / harm-reduction — not a random site that happens to mention weed once.
3. **Authority check** — free tools: Ahrefs Free Backlink Checker, Moz Link Explorer (free tier), or Ubersuggest. Aim for **DR/DA 20+**. Below 15 is usually not worth editorial effort (directories excepted).
4. **Real traffic?** Plug into Similarweb (free) — if it has near-zero traffic, the link is near-worthless.
5. **Spam check** — does the site link out to obvious junk (casinos, pills, link farms)? If the outbound profile is spammy, a link from there can *hurt* you. Skip.
6. **Do they accept your kind of content?** Look for a "Write for us / Gastartikel / Article invité / Schreib für uns / Contact" page. No contact path = no outreach.

## How to go about it — the workflow

### Step 1 — Build a target sheet (1 hour)
Make a Google Sheet with columns:
`Country | Domain | Type (directory/blog/forum) | Contact URL/email | DR | Status | Pitched date | Reply | Link URL | Notes`

Fill it with: all the directory hits from the searches above (Tier 1), plus the named properties you verified (Tiers 2–3).

### Step 2 — Knock out directories first (Tier 1, week 1)
Fast, low-effort, builds your "legitimate business" floor. Submit to every relevant cannabis + local directory. Use **Template B** from [backlinks.md](backlinks.md). Target: 15–20 submissions in week 1.

### Step 3 — Pitch info/advocacy sites (Tier 2)
These won't run an ad, but they'll mention a useful resource. Don't ask for a link directly — offer them a genuinely useful page (e.g. your delivery-info or a guide) as a resource their readers would value.

### Step 4 — Guest-post outreach (Tier 3)
Use **Template A** (Dutch) / **Template D** (German) / and the French equivalent (below). Pitch 2–3 *specific*, original article ideas — not "can I write for you?" Editors say yes to specific ideas, no to vague ones.

### Step 5 — Forums (Tier 4, ongoing)
2–3 substantive answers per week in real threads. Link only when your page is genuinely the best answer. **Never spam** — cannabis communities ban this instantly and it can poison your brand.

### Step 6 — Reclaim unlinked mentions (Tier 5, ongoing)
Monthly, Google: `"bestwiets" -site:bestwiets.com` and `"WietStore" -site:bestwiets.com`. Email anyone who mentioned you without linking — use **Template C**. Conversion rate is 20–40%.

### Step 7 — Track + iterate
Once a month, run Ahrefs Free Backlink Checker on `bestwiets.com`, reconcile against your sheet. Target trajectory: **15 referring domains by month 1, 30–50 by month 3, 50–80 by month 6.** If a month adds <3, your outreach cadence dropped — restart it.

## Realistic outcome

- **Month 1:** 15–20 links, mostly directories. No ranking movement yet — normal.
- **Month 3:** 30–50 referring domains, ideally 2–3 from real cannabis publications (DR 40+). First-page movement on long-tail country/payment/strain terms.
- **Month 6:** 50–80 domains, foundation to start competing for head terms ("wiet kopen", "cannabis online kaufen") in months 9–12.

**The single biggest predictor of success is consistency.** 1–2 outreach actions per week, every week. Sites that stop outreach plateau within ~2 months.
