## Scope decisions (already made)
- **Primary language: Dutch** (NL-first). English at `/en/`, German at `/de/`.
- **No seed keywords / pages.** Catalog has no seed products. Strategy targets the actual products (weed, hash, cali, grit, oil) to avoid doorway/thin pages.
- **No fabricated review schema.** Will be added the moment genuine reviews are collected.
- **Catalog (130 products):** Flower, Cali, Lil Buds, Hash, Grit, Oil, Accessories.

Edit the markdown directly and commit. They are not regenerated by `generate.py`.
