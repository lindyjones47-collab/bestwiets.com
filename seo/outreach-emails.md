# Backlink Outreach Emails — ready to send

Companion to [backlinks.md](backlinks.md) and [backlink-targets.md](backlink-targets.md).
These are **copy-paste ready** guest-post / collaboration pitches in **English, Dutch, German and French**.

**Before you send any of them:**
1. Replace every `[bracket]` — especially `[specific post]` with a real detail from their site.
2. Pitch in the **blog's own language** (use NL for `.nl`, DE for `.de`, FR for `.fr` / French-Belgian).
3. Lead with the free article, not the link. Editors say yes to value, no to "can I have a backlink".
4. Keep the legal wording as written — *"in line with applicable local regulations"* reads credible without a claim you'd have to defend. If you hold a specific licence/registration, name it; it's a strong trust signal.

>
