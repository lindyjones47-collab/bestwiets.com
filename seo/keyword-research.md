# Keyword Research & Competitor Analysis — bestwiets.com

**Scope:** Buyer-intent keywords across Netherlands, Belgium and Germany, mapped to actual catalog (no seeds — catalog has weed, hash, cali, grit, oil).

## How to read this

- **Intent:** **C** = commercial (ready to buy), **I** = informational (researching).
- **Difficulty:** rough estimate. NL "wiet kopen" head terms are **hard** (entrenched competitors with years of authority). Long-tail and country/payment qualifiers are **medium**.
- **Mapped page:** existing page if there is one; otherwise listed as a gap.
- The exact monthly search volumes vary by tool and are not stable enough to commit to numbers in this doc. Use SEMrush/Ahrefs/GSC for volumes; **prioritise by intent quality, not volume**.

## 4. Competitor analysis

Direct organic competitors for the Dutch/Belgian buyer-intent terms generally fall into three groups:

1. **Established NL online cannabis shops** — operating for years, with deep backlink profiles and large catalogues. They dominate head terms like "wiet kopen". Beating them on these terms is unrealistic without 12+ months of authority building. **Compete on long-tail and country/payment qualifiers.**

2. **NL coffeeshop directories** — list multiple physical coffeeshops. Strong on "coffeeshop {city}" terms. Not competing on online-buying intent, so largely irrelevant — but they're good linking partners (see backlinks.md).

3. **DE / BE smaller shops** — newer, weaker backlink profiles, often poor French/multilingual support. **This is where the easiest ranking wins are**, especially `acheter cannabis belgique` and city-specific German queries.

### What competitors do well that we should match
- Detailed product pages with strain genetics, THC %, effects, flavour notes — **our product descriptions are formulaic ("Premium hand-trimmed flower. THC 25–30%."). Expanding them would help product-page rankings.**
- FAQ blocks on product pages — drives schema rich results.
- Real customer reviews on products — boosts CTR and conversion (we can't fake these).

### What competitors do poorly that we already do better
- True trilingual setups with proper `hreflang` — most competitors are NL-only.
- Bilingual Belgium pages (Dutch + French) — almost no competitor has this.
- Clear payment-method targeting (Bitcoin / USDT / bank) — undertargeted by competitors.

## 5. Content gaps (priority order)

1. **3–5 NL strain pages** (Gelato, White Widow, Northern Lights, Sour Diesel) — high intent, lowest difficulty.
2. **2–3 DE city pages** (Hamburg, München, Köln) — low difficulty, geographic intent.
3. **`/hasj-kopen` dedicated page** (currently buried inside `/shop` filter).
4. **`/cannabis-premium-belgique`** — French cluster expansion.
5. **Product description expansion** for top 20 sellers (genetics, effects, flavour profile).
